Discover the new items
on Starbucks' spring menu

Until October of this year, Starbucks Brazil customers can enjoy the new arrivals on the spring menu. There are three products available for a limited time: the return of the iconic Starbucks® Pumpkin Spice line, the launch of the Starbucks® Apple Crisp line, and the special edition of Starbucks® Anniversary Blend 2023 whole bean coffee.

At Starbucks, we believe that every moment is unique and deserves to be cherished. With that in mind, we want to make this campaign even more special as we celebrate the 52nd anniversary of the brand this month. With this commemorative menu, we invite our customers, who are a fundamental part of Starbucks' history, to share this happiness with us and enjoy the season in the best way possible with their favorite drinks.
