StarBucks

Starbucks is a global chain of coffeehouses known for its premium coffee and cozy store ambiance. Founded in 1971 in Seattle, Washington, by Jerry Baldwin, Zev Siegl, and Gordon Bowker, Starbucks has grown to become one of the most recognized and beloved coffee brands worldwide.

What sets Starbucks apart is its commitment to quality and sustainability throughout its entire supply chain. From sourcing ethically grown coffee beans to supporting farmers and communities, Starbucks strives to make a positive impact on both people and the planet. This dedication to social responsibility has earned Starbucks a reputation as a leader in corporate citizenship.
